About us

Liaison Forwarders, headquartered in Kakinada with branches in Mumbai, Visakhapatnam, and Hyderabad along with strong partner agents nationwide, is a renowned Freight and Warehouse Management company. They specialize in various services such as competitive global shipping rates, road transportation, warehousing, ship broking, project cargo logistics, door-to-door service, LCL consolidation, buyers consolidation, coastal shipping, and additional value-added services. With a team of experienced professionals, Liaison Forwarders prioritizes efficient and reliable handling of diverse cargo for customers across industries. Their aim is to cultivate long-term relationships by providing quality services and unwavering reliability. Offering comprehensive logistics solutions under one roof, they streamline the cargo movement process, allowing clients to focus on their core business. Driven by the motto 'Delivering Logistics with Elegance', their commitment revolves around perfection and maintaining elegance in service delivery to ensure customer satisfaction. The company's vision is to emerge as a leading Freight forwarding and Logistic Company in the industry.
